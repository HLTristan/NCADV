Session Description

Survivor and activist Leah Zeiger will share her personal story of forgiveness – how it came into her life, how it changed her body, and how it became a central tenet of her healing and activism. Through a multimedia and interactive presentation, Leah will invite participants to consider: your own relationship to forgiveness, how your body may or may not be a ffected by that relationship, and what might happen if forgiveness should enter your life. Leah will share tools for the practice of forgiveness in personal, relational, and professional settings.

**This session will be interpreted into both ASL and Spanish.**
